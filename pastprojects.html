Past Projects

These are some of the projects I've worked on in the past.

Roll-A-Ball

Roll-A-Ball: A simple tutorial with my own touch added to it.

Skills Learned: Sprints, How to use feedback, basic coding.

Problems: How to balance the game so it was both fun and fair to the player.

Solutions: Having Peers test the game and give me their honest feedback on it and what I could make better.

2D RPG

The 2D-RPG Tilemap game has been the hardest project I've worked on up to date. It touched on Unity's tilemap system, player controller, as well as added a health system and enemy to fight.

The Biggest Challenge: Turn-Based Movement.

Solution: Research Turnstates and how they work to accomplish the turn-based movement I wanted.

Haunted Hospital

The Haunted Hospital was a prebuilt scene I was given and I added my own touches to the scene to make it fit the Haunted vibe.

Skills: Audio Management, Unity Lighting, Unity Timelines.

Problems: Balancing the audios so they didn't drown each other out.

Solutions: A lot of back and forth testing adjusting the audio tracks as well as having peers check out the project and give me their opinion.
